Color Accessibility Information

WCAG AA Compliant Color Combinations

The following color combinations from our palette meet WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards:

Dark Green (#143109) with Off-White (#FAE1DF) Ratio: 12.8:1
Dark Blue (#485696) with Off-White (#FAE1DF) Ratio: 6.2:1
Off-White (#FAE1DF) with Dark Green (#143109) Ratio: 12.8:1

Note: All combinations exceed the WCAG AA requirement of 4.5:1 for normal text and 3:1 for large text, ensuring excellent readability for all users including those with visual impairments.
